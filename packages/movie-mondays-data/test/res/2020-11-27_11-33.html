About the cinema: 

Upgrade your experience at The Kino with our newly installed PREMIUM SEATS! These luxurious fully reclining seats (complete with individual tables and footrests!), are exclusive to our VIP Back row in Cinema 1 and Cinema 2.  To book these seats, simply select the Premium Seat* ticket type when making your booking, only Premium Seats will be made available to select.  Limited in availability, book ahead to avoid disappointment!   

Melbourne’s premiere metropolitan cinema, located at Collins Place in the city’s CBD. The Kino features seven screens, with a superb selection of local and international feature films, documentaries and mainstream titles. Kino offers a stylish café and bar area, ideal for post-film catch ups, film festival soirées, or even hosting your own special events.

For the best value experience with special offers and rewards, be sure to join our Free or Premium Movie Club Memberships.

*Premium seat incurs additional charges. Only available in Cinema 1.  If no premium seat option listed, it is either sold out or not available in selected cinema.

Getting There

Parking

Collins Place Car Park offers Kino Cinema guests free parking during the first two hours of their visit (additional rates apply after two hours). Located at 28 Flinders Lane, the Collins Place Car Park is on the right hand side of the lane, approximately 50 metres from Spring Street. Patrons must validate their parking ticket at the box office for discount. Discount does not apply to prepaid parking.

Wheel Chair & Pram Access

For wheelchair or pram access, enter Collins Place via ramp access on Exhibition St or Flinders Ln. For access from the car park, take the lift to the lower ground level, and then use the ramp down to Kino Cinemas. If you are entering from Collins St, please use the side Sofitel driveway entry and take the central lift to the lower ground level.

Public Transport

Tram routes 109, 11, 12, 48 (Spring St stop). The nearest train stop is Parliament Station.
